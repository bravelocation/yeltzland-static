--- layout: newsstory title: News Story match: Yeltz 3 Bromsgrove 1 ---

Another victory (3-1) against a team who look like they're going down. Goals from Evran, Crispy and Evans means that Bromsgrove are stuck in the bottom four, whereas we're back up to 3rd place.

After a few decent seasons in the Conference, Bromsgrove are now looking like doing a Stafford Rangers and dropping straight through. It makes you wonder whether it's worth going up.

At the top, it's all pretty close again with Merthyr only drawing at Rothwell and Forest Green winning at Hastings.
