--- layout: newsstory title: WHAT A GAME! match: Nuneaton 4 Yeltz 3 ---

Where 7 minutes of injury-time came from I'll never know, but it was a very promising performance under new manager John Chambers.

Such a lot happened in the game I haven't really got space to give the match justice. They took the lead early on from an easy header, and our pre-match half-joking predictions of 0-4 being a good result weren't looking so funny any more. Then into the top corner and we were back in it.
