--- layout: newsstory title: THREE IN A ROW (IT'S NO APRIL FOOL) match: Tamworth 1 Yeltz 2 ---

It's taken some time, but the mighty Yeltz have finally hit some form with three excellent wins in the league. The latest victory was another away victory against a team near the top of the table, a 2-1 win at Tamworth. Goals from Richard Crisp and returning favourite Ian Reed before the break means we're now 10 points above the drop zone and in a healthy 12th place in the table.
