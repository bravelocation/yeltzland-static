--- layout: newsstory title: BRILLIANT! match: Yeltz 3 Salisbury 0 ---

Just like the old days. A dynamic performance from the mighty Yeltz blew Salisbury away to give us some hope (at last) of avoiding the drop.

We started positively right from the start, with young Skidmore and Smithy particularly excellent in the centre of midfield. We had most of the pressure and when headed home from a well-flighted Crispy free-kick.
