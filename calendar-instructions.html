--- layout: default title: Add HTFC fixtures to your calendar ---

If you want the fixture list (including the results) in your calendar, follow the instructions below for your operating system.

If you can't figure it out, try searching for "adding iCal to calendar" to find some suitable instructions

iOS

Instructions from iMore:

  1. Launch the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap on Accounts and Passwords.
  3. Tap on Add Account.
  4. Tap on Other.
  5. Tap on Add Subscribed Calendar under the Other section.
  6. Enter or copy in the server location of the calendar - https://bravelocation.com/automation/feeds/htfc-fixtures.iCal
  7. Turn on 'Use SSL'
  8. Tap the Next button
  9. Tap the Save button

Android/Google calendar

Instructions from Google

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Next to "Other calendars" on the left, click the Down arrow.
  3. Select Add by URL.
  4. Enter the calendar's address in the field provided - use https://bravelocation.com/automation/feeds/htfc-fixtures.iCal
  5. Click Add Calendar. The calendar will appear on the left side under "Other calendars."

Note: It might take up to 12 hours for changes to show in your Google Calendar.
